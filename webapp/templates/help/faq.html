FAQ

If you can't find what you're looking for here send an email to help@twosix.es

Hello. Feel free to peruse this FAQ, but we love hearing from our customers. Please send us an email with your question or fill out this form. Our email is help@twosix.es.

  • Can I just email you my question?

    Of course. Send an email to help@twosix.es.

  • I forgot my password.

    No problem. You want to go here.

  • How do I edit a search term once I've created one?

    The easiest thing to do is create a new search term with the text you want and delete the old one.

  • What is the thumbs down button next to my links for?

    Whenever you get an alert that is junk, click on the thumbs down button next to it. This tells the filter to mark future alerts that look like this one as irrelevant. The more you click thumbs down, the more relevant your alerts will be.

  • Lookout flagged a very important alert as irrelevant.

    Well, this is not good news at all. Send us an email or use this form. Be sure to include the URL of the alert that was irrelevant, (or just copy/paste the text of the alert, we'll figure it out), and the name of the client.

  • I'm getting rubbish alerts in my feed. It's not working!

    Yarg. That's not good. Please send us an email from your account's email address and include which client is getting all the irrelevant alerts and we'll see if we can whip things into shape.

  • How do I contact you?

    The best way to contact us is to email us at help@twosix.es. Or you can use this form. You can also email the owner directly at sean@twosix.es. And you can follow us on Twitter, @twosixesllc
